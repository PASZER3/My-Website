Tooling Engineering & Design

Part Design

DFM Analysis

In-depth Design for Manufacturability evaluation to identify wall thickness variance, draft issues, and ribs to structurally optimize tools and slash costs.

Mouldflow Simulation

Advanced computational processing to simulate physical material execution—accurately predicting filling behavior, air traps, weld lines, and potential warpage.

Mould Design

Robust structural and thermal core/cavity architecture engineered for demanding injection molds and high-output Extrusion Blow Moulding (EBM) tooling setups.

2D/3D Electrode Design

Precise generation and detailing of spark EDM extraction blocks, incorporating exact spark-gap clearances and extraction coordinates for manufacturing.

BOM & Documentation
