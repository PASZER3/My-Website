What We Do

Tooling Engineering
& Design

Leveraging rigorous industrial methodologies — before a single cut of steel is made.

Get a Quote →
🧩

Part Design

Comprehensive 3D modeling for plastic products — fully optimizing for cosmetic specifications and functional geometric requirements. Automotive & commercial grade.

📐

DFM Analysis

In-depth Design for Manufacturability evaluation to identify wall thickness variance, draft issues, and ribs — structurally optimizing tools and slashing production costs.

🌊

Mouldflow Simulation

Advanced computational simulation to predict filling behavior, air traps, weld lines, and potential warpage — before any steel is cut. Save time, save money.

⚙️

Mould Design

Robust structural and thermal core/cavity architecture for both Injection Moulding (IM) and high-output Extrusion Blow Moulding (EBM) tooling setups.

⚡

2D/3D Electrode Design

Precise spark EDM extraction block detailing — incorporating exact spark-gap clearances and extraction coordinates for direct CNC manufacturing.

📋

BOM & Documentation

Error-free production blueprints with detailed Bills of Materials for direct toolroom procurement. 2D drawings, fully dimensioned for machining.
